# Content Corrections

This document outlines the specific content corrections, spelling adjustments, normalization rules, and educational updates applied to the workbook contents.

---

## 1. Subjective Questions Treated as Having One Right Answer
* **Problem**: In the workbook, subjective or opinion-based statements were sometimes paired with rigid "correct" or "incorrect" validation keys (e.g., `العمل الإنساني أهم من الشهرة — غير موافق`).
* **Correction**: Enforced that these are treated as `agree_disagree` or `self_assessment` interactions. The student's choice is saved and marked completed with positive encouragement, rather than being evaluated as academically "right" or "wrong".

---

## 2. Inconsistent Antonyms and Word Associations
* **`معلم / تلميذ`**: While technically complementary roles rather than strict lexical antonyms, they are presented as opposites in the workbook. We treat them as contextual opposites in matching games rather than absolute antonyms.
* **`مكانة / تجاهل`**: Normalized to contrast status (`مكانة` / prestige) with indifference (`تجاهل` / neglect) in the context of learning.
* **`احترام / ازدراء`**: Enforced as a high-quality lexical antonym.
* **`تقدير / إهمال`**: Enforced as a high-quality lexical antonym.

---

## 3. Incomplete Sentences and Paragraphs
* **`ويرسم...`**: In Journey 2 (King of Hearts) retelling text, the sentence ends abruptly with `ويرسم`.
* **Correction**: Completed the paragraph as follows:
  > ...بل لأنه هادئ جداً، ويحب الفقراء، ويعالج الجميع بالمجان، **ويرسم البسمة على وجوه المرضى وأطفال مصر الطيبين بمحبة وإنسانية.**

---

## 4. Title Inconsistencies
* **Problem**: Journey 3 is referred to alternately as `رسالة إلى أبنائنا` (A Letter to Our Children) and `رسالة من آبائنا وأمهاتنا` (A Letter from Our Parents).
* **Correction**: Normalized the title to **`رسالة من آبائنا وأمهاتنا`** to reflect the active voice of the narrator in the listening script.

---

## 5. Gender-Specific Phrasing
* **Problem**: The workbook uses female imperative verbs (e.g., `تخيلي`, `صممي`, `اكتبي`) in some questions, which excludes male students.
* **Correction**: Neutralized all student-facing prompts to use standard inclusive or gender-neutral Arabic terminology:
  * `تخيلي` ➔ `تخيّل` (or `قم بتخيّل`)
  * `صممي` ➔ `صمّم` (or `قم بتصميم`)
  * `اكتبي` ➔ `اكتب` (or `قم بكتابة`)
  * `اقترحي` ➔ `اقترح`

---

## 6. Numbering Typos
* **Problem**: The workbook contains non-sequential numbering (e.g., J1 P131, J2 P194, J3 P110).
* **Correction**: Cleaned database identifiers to follow a logical order, while retaining links to the original workbook page numbers in the inventory mapping.
